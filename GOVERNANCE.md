# Governance

Mnemo is an open-source project founded and maintained by **@torstfugl**.

The official Mnemo project is currently hosted under the **@onemnemo** GitHub organization at:

- `onemnemo/mnemo`

This document explains how project decisions are made for the official Mnemo project.

## Founder-led project

Mnemo uses a founder-led governance model.

The project owner, **@torstfugl**, has final decision-making authority over the official Mnemo project, including but not limited to:

- the official repository and organization
- the website and documentation
- project branding and visual identity
- roadmap and product direction
- architecture and implementation direction
- release timing and release process
- maintainer appointments and removals
- acceptance, rejection, modification, or removal of contributions
- contribution requirements and community rules
- future funding, sponsorship, donation, or hosted-service policies

Contributors are welcome and appreciated, but contributing to Mnemo does not grant ownership, voting rights, release rights, administrative access, maintainership, or decision-making authority over the official project.

## Project changes

The project owner and appointed maintainers may make changes to Mnemo without requiring permission from contributors.

This includes, but is not limited to:

- changing or removing code
- changing or removing features
- redesigning systems
- changing the roadmap
- changing project structure
- changing documentation
- changing contribution rules
- changing release plans
- changing branding, website content, or public messaging

Contributions may be accepted, rejected, modified, reverted, or removed at the discretion of the project owner and maintainers.

## Maintainers

Maintainers may be appointed by the project owner.

Maintainers may help review issues, review pull requests, manage releases, maintain documentation, triage bugs, and guide contributors.

Maintainer status is a responsibility, not ownership of the project. The project owner may add, change, or remove maintainers when needed.

## Contributors

Contributors retain copyright to their own contributions unless otherwise agreed in writing.

By contributing to Mnemo, contributors agree that their contributions may be included in the project and distributed under the project license.

Contributing does not create employment, contractor status, partnership, ownership, equity, revenue share, or a guarantee of future payment.

## Funding and monetization

Mnemo is intended to remain free and open-source.

The project may later receive donations, sponsorships, grants, or revenue from optional hosted convenience services such as managed sync, managed cloud AI, support, or infrastructure.

Core application functionality should not be locked behind a paywall.

Any future funding or revenue-sharing model will require a separate written policy. No current contribution creates a promise of future payment, equity, ownership, or revenue share.

## Forks

The project license may allow others to fork and modify Mnemo.

However, forks are not the official Mnemo project. Forks may not imply that they are official, endorsed, or operated by the Mnemo project unless explicitly authorized.

Use of the Mnemo name, logo, and branding is governed by `BRAND.md`.
