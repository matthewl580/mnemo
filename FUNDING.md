# Funding

Mnemo is a free and open-source project founded and maintained by @torstfugl under the @onemnemo GitHub organization.

Mnemo itself is intended to remain free. Core local application functionality should not be locked behind a paywall.

## Optional paid services

The project may later offer optional paid convenience services, such as:

- managed cloud sync
- managed backups
- managed cloud AI
- hosted infrastructure
- support
- setup assistance
- other convenience services

These services are optional. They are intended to pay for infrastructure, development, maintenance, support, and continued work on Mnemo.

Where practical, users should be able to self-manage equivalent functionality instead of using a hosted Mnemo service.

## Donations and sponsorships

Donations and sponsorships may be accepted through official project channels or maintainer-controlled channels.

Unless otherwise stated in writing, donations are voluntary support for the maintainer and the continued development of Mnemo. Donations do not purchase ownership, governance rights, feature priority, support guarantees, employment, equity, revenue share, or decision-making power.

## Contributors

Contributions are voluntary and do not create a promise of payment.

If Mnemo later receives meaningful funding and decides to compensate contributors, that will be handled under a separate written policy.
